Word of Art

Examples of my favourite paintings

  • Jan Vermeer, The Milkmaid

    The painting shows a milkmaid, a woman who milks cows and makes dairy products like butter and cheese, in a plain room carefully pouring milk into a squat earthenware container on a table.

  • Francisco Goya, Saturn Devouring His Son

    Goya depicts Saturn feasting upon one of his sons. His child's head and part of the left arm have already been consumed.

  • Édouard Manet, The Luncheon on the Grass

    The painting features a nude woman casually lunching with two fully dressed men. Her body is starkly lit and she stares directly at the viewer. .
